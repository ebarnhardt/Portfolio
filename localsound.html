About me

I’ve always enjoyed solving problems on computers. I grew up trouble-shooting issues on friends’ and family members’ computers. Back then I also used computers to create. I made greeting cards for people for years, as well as posters or crude image manipulation.
When I realized I could play, create and problem-solve on computers for a living, I leapt for it and never looked back. I’ve dabbled in poster design, packaging, environmental design and layout. My favorite thing is to craft experiences around a user so I now focus on UI/UX design.
