Mobile App + Site Design

LocalSound

See more live music with this i0s app that shows you a map of all the local shows happening.
The Challenge

Local Music Finder

There are a bunch of great music apps out there that help you do things like listen, create and identify music. There are even some that help you get tickets to concerts when your favorite bands are in town. When it comes to spontaneously seeing live music, however, a good mobile app does not exist. LocalSound is every musiclover’s new companion. It helps you find concerts, big or small, whenever you are in the mood to see live music. Use the music finder map to discover local artists performing at bars, restaurants or any venue around the city any day of the week.
Research + Discovery

Music Lovers

I find persona development to be a great way to kick off a project because it helps me get into the mind of the user right from the start. Going forward, all of my design decisions are based off the goals and frustrations of these personas.

Shawn

Age: 31
Location: Portland, OR
"The Social Musician

Shawn goes to concerts as often as he can. He plays the guitar and many of his friends are musicians. When they go out together, they usually try to find some live music to see. Shawn is always on the hunt for new bands and show experiences. He would like an app that could show him where local artists are playing when he is out and about.

With persona goals and pain points in mind, I created a list of features that the app should have.

Find Nearby Shows

View concerts near a current location - for now or a specified date.

Filter Searches

Filter concert search by genre, venue, area, time of day or price.

Follow Artists

Read about an artist, see their upcoming show and follow your favorites.

Preview Music

Listen to music samples from unknown bands before buying tickets.

See Venue Info

View information about new venues and follow your favorite spots.

Find Nearby Shows

View ticket prices and information, and secure tickets beforehand.

Dance with Friends

Share events or entire calendars with friends to get them to tag along..

Fill Your Calendar

Save events to a calendar to be reminded of shows you want to see.

Sketches

In this phase, I sketched out layout possibilities that could include all of the features discovered with the help of the persona research.

I changed a 3 button layout from the wireframes to one-button to narrow the user’s focus to buying tickets. I split the other two buttons, share and add to favorites, into secondary elements.



Share feature turns into an icon overlaid on the artist’s header image.

Similarly, the add to favorites button becomes less prominent as an icon in the header.



Navigation between the map, list view, concerts, artistis, venues and account information is fixed to the bottom for consistently easy access.

Product Page + Prototype

Promoting the App

I designed a responsive product page for LocalSound to promote its features and functionality as well as provide a link for users to download the app. I also created a working prototype using skeleton to showcase the idea. I am currently running tests to see if this could be a viable product.
